Saylani Aleemiyah University Complex

14 September, 2013
Future Projects
9704

Joint Secretary Mr Amir Madni announced New Future projects in Saylani Annual Symposium 2016 on 13th May , Saylani Aleemiyah University Complex.

On behalf of Saylani Welfare International Trust , all students of Pakistan will be having access to Education , on par with the international standards abroad. Covering a total of 22,000 square yards, the University will be offering  quality education at an affordable level for all students on a national level. This University will be lead by Mohsin-e-Pakistan Dr Abdul Qadeer Khan.
